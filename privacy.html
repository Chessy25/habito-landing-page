Habito respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and what rights you may have regarding your data.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information such as user ID and email address when you sign in.
  • Habits, progress, and completion data created inside the app.

2. How we use your information

  • To sign you in and maintain your account.
  • To sync your data across devices.
  • To provide app features and improve the service.

3. Data storage

Your data may be stored in cloud services such as Firebase or other Google services so it can be available across devices.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with services necessary to operate the app.

5. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may send local notifications on your device. You can disable them at any time in your device settings.

6. Data retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

8. Children

Habito is not intended for children under 13.

9. Contact
