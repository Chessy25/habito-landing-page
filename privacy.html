Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026
Contact: habito.help@gmail.com

Habito respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we may collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: When you sign in (e.g., Google), we receive basic account identifiers needed to create your profile (such as a user ID and email address).
  • App data: Habits and completion data you create in the app (to provide the service).

2. How we use your information

  • Sign you in and keep your account available across devices
  • Sync your habits and progress when you use multiple devices
  • Provide subscription features (Basic/Pro/Lifetime), including purchase verification

3. Data storage

Your account and app data may be stored in cloud services (e.g., Firebase/Google services) so you can access your data on multiple devices.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., authentication, cloud storage, purchase verification).

5. Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may send local notifications on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Data retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by using the “Delete account” option in Settings.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

8. Children

Habito is not intended for children under 13.
