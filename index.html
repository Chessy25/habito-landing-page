About the app

Habito is a minimalist app for creating and tracking daily habits. Designed for people who want to improve their routine without complex settings or unnecessary features.

The app focuses on simplicity, speed, and clarity. Adding new habits is quick, while tracking progress is clear and visually easy to understand.

Whether you want to improve productivity, health, or your daily routines, Habito helps you stay consistent and motivated through small steps every day.
